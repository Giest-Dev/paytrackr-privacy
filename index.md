# PayTrackr Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026

---

# Privacy First

PayTrackr was built around a simple principle:

> **Your financial information belongs to you.**

Your budgets, bills, transactions, savings funds, notes, and other financial information are stored on your device and, if you choose to enable iCloud synchronization, within your personal Apple iCloud account.

Giest Dev does not access, sell, or use your financial information for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

# Information You Enter

PayTrackr allows you to store information such as:

- Budgets
- Paychecks
- Bills
- Transactions
- Savings Funds
- Categories
- Budget Notes
- Reminder Settings

This information is stored using Apple's on-device technologies.

If iCloud Sync is enabled, your information is securely synchronized through your personal Apple iCloud account so it is available across your own Apple devices.

Giest Dev does not have access to your financial records stored within iCloud.

---

# Analytics

To improve PayTrackr, the app uses **Google Firebase Analytics**.

Analytics help us understand how PayTrackr is used so we can improve usability, reliability, and future development.

Examples of analytics events include:

- App opens
- Feature usage (such as creating a bill or savings fund)
- Onboarding completion
- Paywall views
- Subscription purchases
- App version
- Device type
- Operating system version

---

# What We Do NOT Send to Analytics

PayTrackr is intentionally designed so that your financial information is **not transmitted** to Firebase Analytics.

We do **not** send:

- Budget names
- Bill names
- Transaction descriptions
- Notes
- Merchant names
- Account names
- Paycheck amounts
- Bill amounts
- Transaction amounts
- Savings balances
- Budget totals
- User-entered text

Our analytics are designed to measure **how PayTrackr is used**, not **how you manage your finances**.

---

# Crash Diagnostics

PayTrackr uses **Google Firebase Crashlytics** to help identify software crashes and improve application stability.

Crash diagnostics may include technical information such as:

- Device model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Crash logs
- Diagnostic information

Crash diagnostics are used solely to improve the reliability and quality of PayTrackr.

They do not include the financial information you enter into the app.

---

# Subscriptions

PayTrackr Premium is offered through Apple's App Store using Apple's StoreKit framework.

Payments, subscription management, renewals, and cancellations are handled by Apple.

Giest Dev does not receive or store your payment information.

You may manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID subscription settings.

---

# Third-Party Services

PayTrackr uses the following third-party services:

### Apple

- App Store (subscriptions)
- iCloud (optional synchronization)

### Google Firebase

- Firebase Analytics (application usage analytics)
- Firebase Crashlytics (crash diagnostics)

These services are used only to operate, improve, and maintain PayTrackr.

---

# Data Sharing

Giest Dev does not:

- Sell personal information
- Sell financial information
- Share financial information with advertisers
- Share financial information with data brokers
- Build advertising profiles based on your financial data

---

# Data Deletion

You may delete your PayTrackr information at any time by selecting:

**Settings → Reset All Data**

Deleting the app also removes locally stored information from your device.

If iCloud synchronization is enabled, synchronized information may also be managed through your Apple iCloud settings.

---

# Children's Privacy

PayTrackr is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

# Security

PayTrackr relies on Apple's platform security features, including:

- Device encryption
- Face ID
- Touch ID
- Device passcodes
- Secure iCloud synchronization (when enabled)

Users are encouraged to keep their devices and operating systems up to date.
