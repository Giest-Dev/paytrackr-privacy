# PayTrackr Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 2026**

---

## Overview

PayTrackr is a bi-weekly paycheck budget app built with privacy as a foundation. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal or financial information.

All information entered into PayTrackr remains on your device and, if enabled, within your personal Apple iCloud account.

---

## Data Storage

All budget information — including income, paychecks, bills, transactions, and savings funds — is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage systems.

PayTrackr supports optional iCloud synchronization. If enabled, your information is securely synchronized through your personal Apple iCloud account between your own devices.

Giest Dev does not have access to this information at any time.

iCloud synchronization is optional and is never required to use the app.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

* Your name, email address, or personal identifiers
* Bank account information or banking credentials
* Credit card information
* Your physical location
* Device identifiers or advertising IDs
* Usage analytics or behavioral data
* Contacts, photos, calendars, or other personal device information

Information entered into the app remains on your device and, if enabled, within your personal Apple iCloud account. This information is never transmitted to or accessible by Giest Dev.

---

## Third-Party Services

PayTrackr does not use advertising networks or data brokers.

PayTrackr uses Apple's services for:

* App Store subscriptions
* Optional iCloud synchronization

No third-party services are used to collect or monetize user data.

---

## Subscriptions

PayTrackr Premium is offered as an auto-renewing subscription through Apple's App Store.

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at the time of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

You may manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.

Giest Dev does not process payments directly and does not have access to your payment information.

---

## Data Deletion

You may delete all PayTrackr data at any time by using:

**Settings → Reset All Data**

Deleting the app from your device will also remove locally stored information.

If iCloud synchronization is enabled, iCloud data may be managed through:

**Settings → Apple ID → iCloud**

---

## Children's Privacy

PayTrackr is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age. Since information remains on the device and is not transmitted to Giest Dev, no personally identifiable information is collected.

---

## Security

Because your information is stored locally and optionally synchronized through Apple's iCloud services, the security of your data relies on Apple's platform security features and your device's own protections, including passcode, Face ID, and Touch ID.

Users are encouraged to keep their devices and operating systems up to date.

---

Any changes will be reflected by updating the date shown at the top of this page. Continued use of PayTrackr after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
