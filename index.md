# PayTrackr Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

---

## Overview

PayTrackr is a bi-weekly paycheck budget app built with your privacy as the foundation. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal or financial data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

---

## Data Storage

All budget data — including income, paychecks, bills, transactions, and savings funds — is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage system. No data is sent to any server, database, or third party.

**Coming soon — iCloud Sync:** A future update will offer optional iCloud sync. If enabled, your data will sync through your personal Apple iCloud account directly between your own devices. Giest Dev will have no access to this data at any time. iCloud sync will always be opt-in and never required to use the app.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

- Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
- Your financial account information or banking details
- Your location
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Any information you enter into the app

---

## Third Party Services

PayTrackr does not integrate with any third party services, APIs, advertising networks, or data brokers. There are no advertisements in this app. No third party SDKs are used that collect data.

---

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by going to **Settings → Reset All Data** inside the app. Deleting the app from your device also permanently removes all locally stored data. When iCloud sync is added, data stored in iCloud can be managed through your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud.

---

## Children's Privacy

PayTrackr is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from any user of any age, as all data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us.

---

## Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your budget data is protected by your device's own security features including passcode, Face ID, and Touch ID. We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in a future app update, the updated version will be included in the App Store release notes and reflected here with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
